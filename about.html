ABOUT

A refined sanctuary above Kandy’s timeless hills

Tucked into a peaceful, green hillside setting, Arverne Villa Kandy is designed for travelers seeking quiet luxury, thoughtful service, and an intimate boutique villa atmosphere. Enjoy scenic views, calm mornings, and effortless access to Kandy’s iconic Temple of the Tooth.

Wake to birdsong, unwind by the pool, and return to elegant rooms curated for comfort.

Whether you’re here for culture, nature, or pure rest, our villa is a polished base for a world-class Sri Lankan stay.

Experience the perfect blend of quiet luxury and warm hospitality at Arverne Villa. Nestled in the heart of Kandy, we offer a serene boutique escape designed for comfort and refined living.
